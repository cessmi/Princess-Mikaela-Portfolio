The Nativity of Christ

The Nativity of Christ or Belen depicts the scene where the son of God, Jesus Christ was born on Christmas Day.

This piece took a whole month of December, featuring all handcrafted images of those who witnessed the event. Hand painted animals, faces, gold, frankincense and myrrh.

The piece had won “Best in Belen” beating out more than 10 Puroks in Baranggay Catarman.
